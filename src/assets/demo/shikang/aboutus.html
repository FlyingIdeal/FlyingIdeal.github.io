Company Profile

Beijing Mingren Shikang Science&Technology Co.,LTD is located in the beautiful China's capital – Beijing. We are the high-tech enterprise in the science park of Changping district.Our company is a leading global vision health management expert, including the overall solution of theoretical system, method techniques, technical equipments, etc. With the high quality and good effect, our vision rehabilitation equipments are the powerful tools for eyesight health management. We have high standards of production and quality management system to provide reliable and efficient products and services for the users.
Eyesight health management refers to provide the science and long-term management behavior to the users through the reasonable methods, tools and habit cultivation. Our main service objects are low vision population and people who has the risk of vision loss. We can effectively improve eyesight health and make stable long-term vision. Users can get better vision ability and improve the quality of life.
Our products and technologies have been widely used in China mainland, Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan regions. As to the international market, we have entered the Europe and America, the Middle East, Asia and other regions, such as the United States, Russia, Philippines, Malaysia, Chile, Turkey, and so on.
